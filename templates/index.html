About PosturePal

PosturePal is your smart AI-powered fitness coach, offering real-time posture correction, rep counting, and motivation to help you stay active and healthy.

Working

The working behind the application involves the use of some pre-processing methods and a modified version of the 'KNN' algorithm i.e., K-Nearest Neighbors. The algorithm first detects the body landmarks using mediapipe and then converts them into an embedding. This embedding is used against the embeddings of the dataset containing the best pose images and then classifies the user's pose.
